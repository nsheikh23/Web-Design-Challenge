Wind Speed

Wind speed appears to have some correlation with the latitude, but not a strong one. As you move away from the equator, the wind speed increases slightly. Also it can be noted the winds in the Northern Hemisphere are stronger (higher speeds) than the Southern Hemisphere away from the equator. This could possibly also relate to the tilt of the Earth at the time of data collection.
